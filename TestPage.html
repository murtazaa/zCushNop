Both the mat and pocket covers are 100% machine-washable in cold water. You can either throw them in the dryer or let them air-dry.

The dimensions of the mat are 17” X 27" X 1", big enough for your newborn, yet small enough for you to handle comfortably. It weighs less than 3 pounds, perfect for you to carry and support your baby either inside your home or out.

8. When should I start using the zCush Baby Nap Mat?

As soon as you welcome your newborn! Remember that adult supervision is required at all times.

9. Can I continue to use the zCush Baby Nap Mat if my baby starts flipping onto his belly?

Flipping or rolling is a sign that it is time to stop using the zCush Baby Nap Mat. It is important to always position the baby on his back when using the mat. Once your baby starts to flip or roll, please discontinue use.
